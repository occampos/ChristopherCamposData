Optimizing Delivery

Project Statement

AtliQ is a growing FMCG manufacturer, they serve customers across three seperate cities and look foward to expanding further. However, there has been a noticeable trend of key customers opting not to renew their contracts with AtliQ due to service-related issues and so customer service and delivery efficiency has come into question. This has prompted management to prioritize addressing these concerns before expanding to other cities and requested their supply chain analytics team track delivery service levels.

Skills Highlighted

  • Data Cleaning and Exploration with Python
  • Data Visualization with Power Bi (DAX) and Python
  • Machine Learning and Feature Importance with Python (scikit-learn)

Project Description

A comprehensive dashboard was developed with Power BI to enable AtliQ analytics to effectively track delivery metrics and derive data-driven insights. Additionally, a machine learning model and feature importance was employed to pinpoint the root causes of poor delivery metrics and provide actionable recommendations for improvement.
